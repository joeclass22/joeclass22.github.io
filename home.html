About Me

I'm a 3rd year PhD student in linguistics at UCLA. My primary interest is syntax and its interface with morphology. Within this domain, I often find myself investigating the relationship (or lack thereof) between agreement and case. I work mainly within the frameworks of Minimalism and Distributed Morphology (although you could probably convince me to do Nanosyntax).
